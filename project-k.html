ABOUTSTEEL


Our vision at Steel is to be the cornerstone of generational health. We are dedicated to empowering individuals to achieve lifelong wellness through our unique training philosophy. This vision goes beyond the conventional scope of fitness training it’s about laying the foundation for a legacy of health that extends across generations.At Steel, our mission is simple yet profound: To educate, motivate, and transform.


We’ve moved beyond just using off-the-shelf equipment. Understanding the specific needs of our clients, we’ve started manufacturing our own line of training equipment, tailored to support the varied and unique programs we offer.At Steel Personal Training, we use the Dexa Scan for precise body composition analysis—measuring fat, muscle, and bone density—to tailor your fitness plan accurately.
